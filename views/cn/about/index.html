MARCH TO THETOP OF TECHNOLOGY

Kao Fong Machinery Company is the stock public company in Taiwan. Wehave the excellent products image and quality reputation since along time. The scope of company grows every year.

Found in 1968, it has been over 40 years, From the manufacture of traditional milling machine, we increase the product items step by step and induce the Numerical Control category with rich-experience design team and mature production capacity.

The current products include Vertical machining centers, Horizon-tal machining centers and Double column machines etc.

The development of gun drilling machine was due to the request of clients. Our R&D team referred numerous examples and amended the advantages of upgrafing efficiency and enhancing functions to exceed the existed machine models.
